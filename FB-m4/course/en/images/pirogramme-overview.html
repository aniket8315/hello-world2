Programme Overview

Facilitation skills focus on the soft skills of a Trainer which support your day-to-day training seminars to build your confidence as ambassadors of Coty. Facilitation provides a strong fundamental knowledge and practice of the facilitation skills required for training and engaging participations in all situations.

This programme is a mix of theory and practice.

Theory

  • To attend several different scenarios (in-salon trainings, studio seminars, internal meetings) to discover and complete the knowledge online and offline.
  • To search online resources to gain a deeper understanding and knowledge where it's called out as a specific task.

Practical

You need to complete:
  • Videos of yourself completing the required tasks.
  • Put into practice your new knowledge.
  • Capture/collect your progress and provide feedback on required tasks.
  • Upload and familiarise yourself with the apps.
